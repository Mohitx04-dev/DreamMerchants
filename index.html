About Us

DREAM MERCHANTS-VIT is a club that intends to provide a platform for future leaders and corporate captains. Our objective is to develop an interest among individuals in the field of finance, business, and entrepreneurship. We aim to provide opportunities for students to explore a career in Finance and Trade while understanding the relationship between business and economic disciplines.
